ACADEMICS

DAYCARE

OUR DAYCARE ACCEPTS KIDS FROM 10 MONTHS AND ABOVE. THE PROGRAM TAKES CARE OF THESE KIDS FIVE DAYS A WEEK FROM 8 AM TO 5 PM.

KINDERGARTEN ONE

AT THIS STAGE, THE KIDS ARE TAUGHT BASIC NUMERACY AND WRITING SKILLS. THEY LEARN IN A WARM WELL VENTILATED ENVIRONMENT UNDER THE CARE OF QUALIFIED PROFESSIONALS.

KINDERGARTEN TWO

BEFORE THE KIDS START GRADE SCHOOL, THEIR KINDERGARTEN YEARS ARE ENRICHED WITH MANY EDUCATIONAL ACTIVITIES LIKE DRAMA, PROBLEM SOLVING, READING, AND FUN COMPETITIONS.

GRADE ONE

WITH A CLASS CAPACITY OF AT MOST 30 STUDENTS, OUR KIDS ARE TAUGHT BY 2 DEDICATED TEACHERS WHO GIVE THEM THE BEST SPEACIALIZED EDUCATION TO MATCH THEIR LEANING NEEDS.

GRADE TWO

WE EXPOSE OUR STUDENTS TO THE COMPLEX WORLD OF TECH. THEY ARE TAUGHT ALL THE FUNDAMENTAL OF COMPUTER AND THE INTERNET. THIS PREPARES THEM FOR ADVANCES TRAINING IN THEIR LATER YEARS.

GRADE THREE

KIDS AT THIS LEVEL OF EDUCATION ARE PREPARED FOR THE COMPLICATED PROCESS OF HAVING MULTIPLE SUBJECT TEACHERS AND ALSO TAKING MORE ACTIVE ROLES IN THE CLUBS AT THE SCHOOL

GRADE FOUR

OUR 4TH GRADERS ARE ACTIVE LEADERS IN THE SCHOOL. THEY ARE TAUGHT TO BE BRILLIANT IN THE ARTS, SCIENCE, MATHS, AND TECHNOLOGY

GRADE FIVE

OUR STUDENTS ARE PREPARED TO BE RESPONSIBLE LEARNERS AND ALSO GOOD CITIZENS. THEIR CURIOSITY IS NURTURED AND THEY ARE CHALLENGED TO SOLVE REAL WORLD PROBLEMS IN ALL OF THEIR LESSONS.

GRADE SIX

OUR SENIORS LEAVE FBFS WITH A STRONG APPRECIATION OF GOOD LEADERSHIP TENETS AND FIRM FOUNDATION IN MATHS, SCIENCE, TECHNOLOGY, AND THE ARTS.
