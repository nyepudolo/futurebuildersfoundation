Meet Our Staff

Mrs. Yeah T. Garteh; Proprietress

Mrs. Yeah Garteh is an esteemed educator, boasting over three decades in pedagogy, classroom coordination, and educational leadership. Before founding the Future Builders Foundations School System, she held the esteemed position of Registrar at Solid Foundation School. Under her guidance as the Proprietress, the school transformed from a humble study group to a modern facility that now serves over 200 students in the Cow Field Community of Duport Road. Mrs. Garteh proudly holds a Bachelor's Degree in Education from the University of Liberia, earned in 2005.

Mr Daniel W. Garteh; Principal

Mr. Garteh boosts over a decade of professional experience in School Administration. He is also a former Adjunct Faculty of the University Of Liberia. Mr. Garteh is an esteemed Civil Servant and has worked over a decade as the Head of the Population Policy Coordination Unit at the defunct Ministry of Planning and later, at the LIberia Institute for Statistics and Geo-Information Services(LISGIS). In this post, he participated in several high-level panels globally and locally on Population Policies and also coordinated several Government's efforts to enhance a sustainable population growth. AS the Principal of FBFS, Mr. Garteh dedicates his time to the progressive growth of the school by directing investments towards state-of-the-art educational facilities.

Mr. Akoi A. Buway; Vice Principal for Academic Affairs

Mr. Buway is a trained Teacher with over a decade of experience in hands-on classroom management. Mr. Buway has dedicated most of his Professional life to the training and development of the minds of Young Liberians. After joining the staff of the FBFS, he has contributed to several initiatives of the school such as running co-cirricular activities like the Spelling-bee, Quizzing and Press Clubs with enormous success. As the Vice Principal, Mr. Buway ensures the sound compilation of the Teacher's teaching materials, while also carrying out thorough monitoring and supervision of all academic activities.

Mulbah Gayflor; Dean of Students

Mr. Gayflor is a dedicated member of our staff. Since he joined our team almost half a decade ago, he has served as one of our most hardworking teachers and also on with an extremely fruitful relationship withh the students. As a career Educator, Mr. Gayflor is responsible for instilling discipline and adherence to the School's ethics code by the Pupils.

Mrs. Vesta Warbey, Senior Consultant

Mrs. Warbey is a career Social worker with over 10 years of experience coordinating social welfare activities at the Ministry of Gender, Children and Social Protection. A 2008 graduate of the University of LIberia, Mrs. Warbey has worked with several internation NGO's. She advises the school on crisis management between the school and Parents and also the comunity. She also recommends practices that could make the working environment at the school more conndusive and welcoming.

D. Woah Garteh, Jr.

Mr. Garteh is an celebrated Poet with several Awaards under his belt. A Chemistry major at the University of Liberia, Mr. Garteh has led a career that includes several voluntary initiatives at different children-centered institutions like the Don Bosco Youth Center. He also currently serves on the Youth Advisory Panel of the United Nations Women (UN Women). At the FBFS, he advises the school on the workings of all of it's Co-cirricular activities.
